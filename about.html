About the company

Local land development with a direct, people-first sales approach.

Tui Nabou Development & Construction Pte Limited is a Fiji-based property development company committed to creating high-potential residential opportunities in strategically located growth areas. Our focus is on delivering development-ready land that offers practical value, long-term security and a clear path to ownership for homebuyers and investors alike.

At the heart of our work is the Natogadravu, Wainibokasi development, ideally positioned near Nausori Airport and just minutes from Nausori Town. This location reflects our vision of offering land in accessible and fast-developing areas where families can build with confidence and investors can secure lasting value.

What sets our development apart is its readiness. The subdivision is planned to support immediate residential use, with key infrastructure and services already considered as part of the offering. This includes piped water, electricity connection, tar-sealed roads, underground fire hydrants, open recreational space and surveyed 99-year TLTB lease titles. With lot sizes ranging from approximately 800 to 1,300 square metres, the project is designed to meet the needs of both first-home buyers and those seeking a solid land investment.

We believe property development should be both professional and personal. That is why our approach is built around direct communication, honest guidance and responsive service. From the first enquiry through to the decision-making process, we aim to make every interaction straightforward, informative and supportive.

Leadership & contacts

Management team

SM

Mr Semi Matalau

Managing Director Company leadership Phone: +679 900 9484

Mr. Semi Matalau serves as the Managing Director of TUI NABOU DEVELOPMENT & CONSTRUCTION LTD, where he provides strategic leadership in advancing land development initiatives with professionalism, integrity, and a deep understanding of community and infrastructure needs. With a distinguished background spanning traditional leadership, public service, and regional development, he brings exceptional insight and credibility to projects involving land use, subdivision, and property development.

As Turaga Na Tui Nabou of Nuku District, Tailevu, Chairman of the Tailevu Provincial Council, and a former civil servant who has held several senior public appointments, including Chairman of the Central Division Drainage Board and Community Policing Eastern Division, Mr. Matalau has earned a strong reputation for trusted leadership, sound judgment, and practical engagement in matters affecting land, communities, and sustainable development.

Through TUI NABOU DEVELOPMENT & CONSTRUCTION LTD, Mr. Matalau is committed to working closely with landowners and key stakeholders to unlock the value of land through responsible subdivision and development. His leadership is defined by a clear vision for progress, a commitment to sound planning, and a focus on delivering long-term value for landowners, communities, and future generations.
