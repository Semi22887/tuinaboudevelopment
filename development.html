Buyer FAQ

Fast answers for interested buyers

Why is this location attractive?

The subdivision is near Nausori Airport and Nausori Town, inside the growing Suva–Nausori corridor where serviced land is becoming harder to find.

Who is this suited for?

It is especially ideal for first-home buyers seeking an accessible pathway into property ownership, families wanting generous lot sizes for future home construction, and investors looking for long-term value in the fast-growing Suva–Nausori corridor.

What services are already in place?

The development is already supported by key essential services and infrastructure, including piped water supply, electricity connection, tar-sealed roads, underground fire hydrants, and dedicated recreational open space. The subdivision is also presented with surveyed and registered 99-year TLTB lease titles, giving buyers added confidence in the readiness and structure of the offering.

Why act now?

Opportunities like this are limited. With ready-to-build residential lots in a completed subdivision, buyers have the advantage of securing land in a well-connected location near Nausori Airport and Nausori Town without waiting on future infrastructure delivery. The combination of serviced lots, practical amenities, and strong location appeal makes this an attractive option for both homebuyers and investors.
